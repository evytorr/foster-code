Contact Us


We serve young teens of color between the 7th and 11th grade who are from low income communities. Since 2009, we've used the concept of developing strong teams as a way for our young men to learn about collaboration, ideation, and long lasting life skills. In small teams, our youth learn the process of taking an idea to the market.
